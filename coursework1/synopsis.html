Synopsis

Nicholas Angel a talented London police officer is sent to the country town of Sandford by his jealous officers. Nicholas now has to adjust to life in a peaceful setting where the biggest crimes are missing swans. But not everything is as it seems when people turn up dead in mysterious accidents.

    Director: Edgar Wright

    Genres: action,mystery, thriller, comedy

    Rating: 15

    Release date: 14 Febuary 2007

    Box office: 80.7 million USD
